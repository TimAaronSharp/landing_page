ABOUT ME

I love creating fun, interactive experiences for people to enjoy. Building applications that elicit smiles and laughter fills me with great satisfaction. Likewise, the feeling of overcoming tough coding challenges rewards a great feeling of accomplishment.

This feeling keeps bringing me back to challenge myself to increase my skillset, expand my knowledge, and to build increasingly higher quality applications as efficiently as possible.
